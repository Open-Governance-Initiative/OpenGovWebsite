What is OpenGov Initiative
The OpenGov Initiative also known as Open Governance initiative is a community of citizens whose aim is to drive transparency, accountability and inclusion in governance.
This means any Nigerian can be part of this movement to drive a postitve change in this country, irrespctive of your gender, tribe or religion.
Our story
The Open Governance initiative was born out of the events of the EndSars protest, where we witnessed Nigerians stand up to demand for justice irrespective our ethnic or religious beliefs.

We witnessed a new Nigeria, a Nigeria bounded in unity, a Nigeria where it didn't matter whether you were Igbo, Yoruba or Hausa, a Nigeria where the Muslims and Chritains saw eachother as brothers and sisters rather than rivals, a Nigeria that you and I were proud of.

We would forever rememeber 20-10-2020, the day peaceful protesters where being gunned down by men in uniform, while singing tha national anthem and waving the natonal flag, a day some of us now refer to as the BlackTuesday.

We would never forget that day, we would never forget the inncoent souls that gave their lives for the liberation of this country, we would never forget the day we truly gained our independence as a country.

This is how the Open Governance Initiative was born, born not out of pain but in hope that one day Nigeria would become a great country we would like to call home.
Our Philosophy
Accountabilty

We believe in accountability, we believe leaders are to serve the people and not themselves or the interest of their parties.
Transparency

Transparency is the only way government can gain the trust of the people which will in turn reduce the rate of corruption in the country.
Collaboration

Every citizen is a stakeholder of this country, that's why we all need to come together and get involved in governance.
Technology

With technology we can drive all the 3 philosopy mentioned above and work toward creating a better Nigeria.
The reformation of Nigeria is going to be as a result of our collective efforts as Nigerians.
Our Vision
The vision of the Open Governance Initiative is to eradicate bad governance, promote nation building and enourage collaboration between citizens and government.

We believe Nigeria has what it takes to become a first world country but unfortunately over the years our resources has been mismanaged by corrupt politcians.

Therefore, it's the responsibility of every Nigerian to get involved in fixing Nigeria, this is the time to rise up and take back our country, we can no longer be passive about national development.
Our Mission
This movement is centered on restoring balance to our country Nigeria and some of the ways we aim to achieve that is by educating the masses so they can make an informed decision when voting, ensuring elected leaders are transparent and being held accountable in office.

The Open Governance Initiative is not a stand alone entity that's why we always seek partnerships with other organizations for the sake of driving the grand mission of national development.

Our mission is to see competent and trustworthy leaders take postions in government offices, and as a result make Nigeria great again!
